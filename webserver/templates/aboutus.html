The Siblings/FOUNDER’S

The Shari foundation was created by Amire, Ta'naya, and Craig Miller. In honor of their loving mother's memory. Their mother Shari Mayes-Miller was diagnosed with cervical cancer and passed away at the age of 28, in February of 2014. The purpose of the newly developing foundation is to spread awareness, share their mother's story, and to help other families who may have a similar experience. Help us spread awareness by just sharing this website, follow on all social media page’s, and contribute to help out with this beautiful journey.
